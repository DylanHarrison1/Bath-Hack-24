Welcome to the website of:

Mike Fulham - The License Plate Man

Hey there folks, I'm Mike and like many others in the world, I'm a long time fan of license plates. I run this blog to keep all of my fellow adventurers in plating updated on the latest hot news in the license plate world.

When I'm not collecting and cataloging plates, I'm usually spending time driving with my plates or enjoying a nice cold one, although of course not at the same time!!!!!! Of course, that's not to mention the treasured time I spend with my beautiful wife and two daughters, who I love more than anything in the world. Anyway, I hope I can bring the kind of joy to you through my blog that my license plates have brought to me! As always, stay plating!

- Mike
